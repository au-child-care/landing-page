child development porfolio made easy!

crayoncamp is a platform for educators to conveniently document child observations, developmental milestones, and intentional teaching plans.

Learn more

Features

Role-Based dashboards

Each type of user has its own dashboard which shows targeted visualizations through data analytics.

 

Smart observation logging

Educators will benefit from Machine Learning-powered EYLF outcome recommendations whenever they log observations.

 

Easy milestones tracking

Each child's milestones (based on EYLF) are presented in a checklist and are grouped by age and developmental area.

 

Intentional teaching plans

Educators can plan and collaborate activities that promotes the children's developmental growth.

 

Parents and guardians' involvement

Administrators can create account for the parents and guardians to access their child(ren)'s portfolio.

 

Free!

crayoncamp is free to use! A centre's administrator just need to sign up and once given an account, he / she can start logging in and setup accounts for his / her centre's educators and parents and guardians.
